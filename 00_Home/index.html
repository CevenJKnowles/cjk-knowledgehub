    CJK KnowledgeHub¶

    Welcome to the CJK KnowledgeHub — my living workspace for learning, experimentation, and portfolio building in AI, prompt engineering, and digital systems design.

    This site hosts my projects, personas, workflows, coursework, and reference knowledge. It’s both my personal lab notebook and the backbone of my future professional portfolio.

    What this hub is for¶

    • Documenting my journey from beginner to working AI professional
    • Building reusable workflows for prompting, evaluation, and reasoning
    • Capturing course notes and exercises from my ongoing learning paths
    • Designing and stress-testing the Io Persona, my long-term AI collaborator concept
    • Maintaining a structured knowledge base I can grow and refine over time

    If you’re a reviewer, recruiter, or collaborator, this hub shows how I think, how I organize complexity, and how I learn in public.

    Io Persona — Flagship Project¶

    The Io Persona is my long-term flagship: a designed AI “collaborator” with explicit protocols, modes, and evolution across versions (IO-I → IO-II → IO-III).

    Inside this section you’ll find:

    • Overview: the concept, purpose, and evolution timeline
    • IO-I (v1.0–v1.2): early experiments and blueprint drafts
    • IO-II (v1.2–v1.4 dev): current active persona with richer protocols and alignment rules
    • IO-III (v0.1 concepts): early thinking about future, multi-agent, local, and embodied directions
    • Experiments: scratchpads and test ideas that don’t yet belong in a formal blueprint

    This project connects prompt engineering, AI safety/ethics, UX, and systems design into one evolving artefact.
