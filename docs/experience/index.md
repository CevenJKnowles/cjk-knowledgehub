# Experience

The three roles below span 26 years of professional practice across
commercial production, independent creative work, and operational
management. The domains differ. The working method does not:
define scope, make decisions explicit, deliver to standard.

---

## Conceptor & Project Manager

**Simpleshow GmbH** · Freelance · Berlin, DACH and UK · Nov 2020 – Dec 2024

Creative lead and project manager for global clients in the banking,
pharmacology, automotive, and tech industries, delivering explainer
videos and interactive learning modules across multi-stage production
pipelines.

- Directed cross-functional teams from brief through to final delivery
  across distributed client and agency stakeholder environments
- Managed concurrent projects across DACH and UK markets, maintaining
  consistent delivery standards under sustained commercial pressure
- Operated the Simpleshow Videomaker platform at expert level: a
  proprietary AI-assisted video production tool used for script
  development, motion graphics, brand implementation, revision cycles,
  and final delivery
- Worked to strict CI and compliance requirements on behalf of
  international A+ clients, coordinating illustration, motion design,
  and sound design across each production
- Stepped into senior project management responsibilities when required,
  maintaining timeline and quality standards through complex feedback
  rounds

**Self-employed** · International · May 1999 – Dec 2024

Independent practice across digital, web, video, and live-media formats,

- Delivered end-to-end across the full project lifecycle: scoping,

  formats, environments, and team configurations

  email marketing system, including segmented campaigns and

  plans as a consistent practice
- Approximately 500 projects delivered across digital, video, web, and
  live-media formats
